\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the governing body responsible for managing the city. 

The \doxylink{class_government}{Government} class is responsible for overseeing the city\textquotesingle{}s resources, citizens, utilities, and various actions to enhance the city\textquotesingle{}s growth and functionality. It manages the creation of buildings, utility services, taxation, and responses to natural disasters.
